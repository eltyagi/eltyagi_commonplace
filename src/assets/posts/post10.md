---
title: Letters Never Sent
classification: Poetry
excerpt: Words written in the dark, meant for eyes that will never see them.
date: 2025-11-25
---

I wrote you letters in the dark,
Words that burned but left no mark.
Folded twice and tucked away,
Things I meant but couldn't say.

Years have passed, the ink has dried,
Still those words remain inside.
Perhaps someday I'll set them free,
But for now, they stay with me.
