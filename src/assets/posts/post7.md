---
title: Echoes in the Valley
classification: Poetry
excerpt: A meditation on solitude and the voices we carry within.
date: 2025-11-10
---

In the valley where shadows sleep,
I found the words I couldn't keep.
They echoed off the ancient stone,
Reminding me I'm not alone.

The mountain stands, unmoved by time,
While I search for words that rhyme.
But meaning lives between the lines,
In spaces where the silence shines.
